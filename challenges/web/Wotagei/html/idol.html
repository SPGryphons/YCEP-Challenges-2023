Summary of the story:

"Oshi no Ko" is a manga series written by Akasaka Aka and illustrated by Mengo Yokoyari. The story follows Nagisa Kisaragi, a young girl who is a fan of the idol group "LIP x LIP." Nagisa has a deep admiration for the group's members, Yujiro and Aoi, and dreams of becoming an idol herself.

One day, Nagisa receives a mysterious invitation to a fan event for LIP x LIP. She attends the event and is shocked to discover that Yujiro and Aoi are not only real people, but they also possess supernatural abilities. Yujiro has the power to make people fall in love with him, while Aoi has the ability to read minds.

As Nagisa becomes more involved with LIP x LIP, she learns that they are not ordinary idols, but part of a larger conspiracy involving their supernatural powers. Nagisa becomes determined to uncover the truth behind the mystery and protect her beloved idols.

Along the way, Nagisa faces various challenges and obstacles, including rival idol groups, powerful organizations, and personal struggles. She forms new friendships, uncovers secrets about the past, and grows as a person as she navigates the complex world of idols and supernatural abilities.

As the story progresses, Nagisa's bond with Yujiro and Aoi deepens, and she becomes more entwined in the fate of LIP x LIP. The story delves into themes of friendship, love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of dreams, as Nagisa fights to protect her oshis (favorite idols) and uncover the truth behind their powers.

Overall, "Oshi no Ko" is an engaging and emotional story that combines elements of the idol genre with supernatural elements, creating a unique and compelling narrative. It explores the world of idols beyond the glitz and glamour, delving into the challenges and sacrifices that idols and their fans may face in pursuit of their dreams.
